Our Curriculum

Our Curriculum Is Divided Into 3 Stages:

Stage 1:

->English, Numbers And Algebra, Diversity of Living and Non-Living Things, Cycles in Plants and Animals (Life Cycles), Human System, Plant System, Cell System, Knowing Myself, Others and My Surroundings

Stage 2:

->Diversity of Materials, English Grammer, Character & Citizenship Education (CCE), Cycles in Matter and Water, Electrical System, Coming Together as a Nation

Stage 3:

->Reasoning, Communication and connections, Business Skills, Interaction of Forces, Interactions within the Environment, Energy Forms and Uses, Understanding Our Past, Energy Conversion
